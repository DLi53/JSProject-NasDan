Stock Info






  • Hi, Welcome to NasDan!

  • NasDan is a Stock Data Visualizer where you can see the historical data of a stock.
  • To begin, please type in a stock ticker symbol (Ex. AAPL, MSFT, TSLA) to view the historical chart.
  • You can toggle the chart type to a bar graph by clicking the "Toggle Graph" button.
  • You can also change the dates of the data you are viewing by pressing one of the buttons below the graph.

  • Enjoy!
